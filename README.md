## üìä Dashboard Insights
1. Sales by State

* California leads in total sales, while other states show moderate performance.

* State-level trends help identify key markets for strategic focus.

2. Customer Segment Analysis

* Consumer segment dominates sales (51%), followed by Corporate (30%) and Home Office (19%).

* Understanding segment distribution supports targeted marketing.

3. Top 10 Products by Sales

* Canon imaging systems are the top-selling products.

* Followed by Fellowes and Cisco products.

* Insight into top products aids inventory and marketing strategies.

4. Sales by Category & Sub-Category

* Furniture: Chairs and Tables are the dominant sub-categories.

* Office Supplies: Storage and Binders lead, with notable sales for Appliances and Paper.

* Technology: Phones and Machines drive sales, with Accessories and Copiers contributing significantly.

5. Monthly Sales Trends

* Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology exhibit seasonality, with peaks in the last quarter of the year.

* Helps anticipate demand and optimize stock.

6. Delivery Mode Performance

* Standard Class dominates with over 1.3M in sales.

* Followed by Second Class and First Class deliveries.

* Insights into delivery modes support logistics and fulfillment planning.
